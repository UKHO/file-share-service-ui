Exchange sets

Update your ENCs for a vessel by making an exchange set

Your AVCS exchange set will contain the latest editions and all the updates available for your requested ENCs. Some may be more up to date than those available in the AVCS Weekly service.

Please note - Users may experience longer waiting times when creating exchange sets during times of high demand. High demand currently occurs Thursday early evenings or Friday mornings. This causes small exchanges sets to take 4 minutes plus to create. Future improvements are in progress to resolve this issue.

We apologise for any inconvenience caused.

You can add a single ENC or upload a list.
